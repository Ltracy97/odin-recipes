Easy Lasgna

Ingredients:

  • Beef: This easy lasagna starts with ground beef. You can use Ground Turkey as a lighter option.
  • Spaghetti sauce: Use store-bought or homemade sauce.
  • Cheese: you will need cottage cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  • Eggs: Eggs help bind the cheese mixture together,plus, they end moisture and richness.
  • Seasonings: Season the easy lasagna with parsley, salt, and black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles: Of course you will need these
  • Water: Pour 1/2 cup of water around the edges of the baking dishe before baking.

Steps:

  1. Cook and drain the Ground beef, then stir Spaghetti sauce and simmer
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the block of Parmesan, and seasonings
  3. Spread 3/4 cup of sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles, 1 3/4 cups of cheese mixture, and 1/4 cup sauce; repeat layers once more. Top with remaining 3 noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese. Pour 1/2 cup water along the edges of the dish. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
